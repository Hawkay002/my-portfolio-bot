Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for the Shovith.dev Security Bot. This bot is designed to provide secure verification services for accessing restricted projects on the Shovith.dev portfolio.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of data required to verify your identity and prevent abuse. This includes:

  • Telegram User ID: To uniquely identify your session.
  • Username & Name: To address you correctly and maintain audit logs.
  • Phone Number: To verify you are a real human and not an automated script.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Identity Verification: To generate a One-Time Password (OTP) that allows you to download secure files.
  • Security Logs: We maintain a record of verified downloads to prevent misuse of sensitive tools (e.g., Unredactor, Steganography tools).
  • Spam Prevention: To ensure that download requests originate from legitimate users.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in our private Google Firestore database.
  • We do not store your data on any public servers or third-party advertising platforms.
  • Temporary verification sessions are deleted immediately after the verification process is complete or expires.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties, advertisers, or external organizations.

5. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to request the deletion of your verification logs from our database at any time.
